About
Jessie Juarez is a bilingual Video Editor and Director of Photography with 6+ years of experience spanning commercial production, broadcast, and live events.

Based in Los Angeles, he has led multi-platform campaigns across 21+ television markets and directed high-profile Spanish-language broadcast content — including production for the National Radio Spanish Broadcast for Super Bowl LX.

His work spans the full production lifecycle, from concept and pre-production through post-production delivery, with a proven track record of helping clients secure six-figure contracts.

Fluent in English and Spanish, Jessie brings both creative vision and operational precision to every project.
